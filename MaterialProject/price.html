Price of an Asp Explorer

Using a conversion rate of $1000 to 1 Cr, a well fitted Asp Explorer is worth ~$25,000,000,000. This conversion rate is derived from the fact that 1 ton of gold only costs ~50,000 Cr but ~$50,000,000. However, It is recommended to have a Guardian Frame Shift Drive Boster as well, which is created from priceless Guardian artifacts. See here for more

This ship should have a jump range of ~60Ly which should be enough to get you to interesting systems in only a few hours.
